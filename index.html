Love padlocks enable us to leave behind tokens of our love and affection forever. They are around us. Why shouldn't we build a site and an application that will serve us forever with the same purpose with infinte capacity?
Process of buying and attaching padlock will be very realistic allowing you to customize your padlock in arbitrary manner. Padlocks will be inspected to prevent offensive messages.
You will be able to look around the virtual padlock bridge, zoom in and out of several level of zoom, inspect padlocks and their public content, socialize on the virtual padlock bridge.
Proposed concept is similar to One million dollar page with a social twist.

You will be able to:

  • buy padlocks with different designs,
  • attach photos - both private and public,
  • schedule emails in distant future - to your loved ones or to yourself,
  • browse virtual padlock bridge with many levels of zoom,
  • search virtual padlock bridge.

    • You will NOT be able to:
  • destroy or delete your padlock - it will be with us forever.
